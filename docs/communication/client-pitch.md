# Client Pitch

## Simple Explanation

AI Workflow Automation Dashboard is an example of how a business can turn repetitive manual work into a structured internal tool.

Instead of handling requests through scattered emails, spreadsheets or documents, the tool captures the request in a form, generates a draft, lets a human review the output and keeps the final version in history for traceability.

The AI behavior in this portfolio project is mock-based, but the workflow shows how a real AI provider could be integrated later in a controlled and reviewable way.

## Business Problems This Type of Solution Can Help With

- Repetitive documents
- Manual reports
- Client responses
- Internal request tracking
- Process summaries
- Administrative workflows

## Example Services This Project Supports

- AI Workflow Audit

## Discovery Questions

- What repetitive process takes the most time each week?
- Where do requests currently arrive?
- What information is copied manually?
- What outputs are generated repeatedly?
- Who reviews or approves the final result?
- What would need to be tracked in history?

## Soft CTA

If your team has a repetitive workflow that depends on scattered notes, manual copying or inconsistent documents, we can map the process and identify where a structured internal tool or AI-assisted workflow could help.
